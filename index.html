About the Celebrant

Ubong James is a committed, dedicated and a versatile ICT personnel. He is an indigene of Mbiabong Etoi, Uyo Local Government Area, Akwa Ibom state, Nigeria. He studied Computer at Deword ICT Academy where he obtained a Diploma in Information Technology in 2016 from there he went on and to expand the scope of his studies in ICT. He has served & taught in several Tech Organization/Institutes, he has trained over 500 students in ICT and still impacting. According to Him, ICT is his Calling and as such he is doing it as a ministry. He is currently pursuing his Vision (To Establish an ICT Centre where Many Nigerians will be equipped with UpToDate ICT Skills) which was first conceived in 2018 and started manifesting in 2020 as this is the birth of UBTECH Media Group which has the likes of other ICT-driven youths.
